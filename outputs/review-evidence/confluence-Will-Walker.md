I cursed Jannie mentioning this in today's management meeting, because the EY demo this afternoon was a disaster. That disaster is easily avoided by actioning this proposal. This risk has been raised for a number of years. CWUK are Cloud first, and must get this done. 

Aim -  Move Key Sales and PM Demo firms to Production. 

Summary Benefits
1) Most stable and best performance.    Products will demo in best  attractive light. 
2) Redundancy.  Option to demo on Production or Release.  
3) Key Demos eg AccountEx cause significant delay for the product production teams 

I’m aware of at least 2  key  failed PM demos to EY due to current demo firm location this year. 

Current Practice 
Sales, PM and EMT demo on the firms  below. 

Release Branch  -  unstable due to not production ready code.
Sales   https://uk.cwcloudpartner.com/leaderco 
PM      https://uk.cwcloudpartner.com/pminnovations 

EMT use 2 firms  “training” and “education” on Release branch  and  Production respectively.   Excellent. 
https://uk.cwcloudpartner.com/training 
https://uk.casewarecloud.com/education/ 

COSTS of moving main demo firms to production are minimal. 
Minimal Sever costs   Usage is low.  

Firm Creation and Product switch on is easy. 

Main cost is time to create data suitable for demoing. This is an opportunity for Sales and PM to build “clean” demo firms.
 a) Other teams could assist.
i)  eg manual creation by QA or Support.
ii) Development could assist, but concentrating on product development likely higher priority. eg
a) Existing SE Engagements copied from LeaderCo and  PMIInovations.  
b) Use Collaborate APIs to create “Entitles,  Users, Contacts”   Easy - and  would save many teams time long term.   A worthwhile future task. . 

Downsides
